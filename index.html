About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Computer Science at Kansas State University. I earned my Ph.D. & M.Tech (Research) in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. My research lies at the intersection of formal methods, program verification, and AI/cyber-physical system safety. I also have strong teaching experience in programming, algorithms, and automata theory.

Get in touch Publications

Research Vision

I aim to develop scalable formal verification frameworks for AI-enabled cyber-physical systems. My work bridges symbolic reasoning and data-driven perception models to ensure safety and reliability of neural network–controlled systems such as autonomous vehicles and intelligent power grids.

Long-term, my goal is to unify logic-based verification, simulation analysis, and neural approximation techniques into practical certification tools for trustworthy autonomous systems.
