About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Computer Science at Kansas State University, working with Prof. Pavithra Prabhakar. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore>, under the supervision of Dr. Deepak D’Souza. My research focuses on formal methods, program verification, and the safety of AI-enabled cyber-physical systems.

I develop frameworks that combine logical reasoning and data-driven models to verify systems that integrate perception and control. I am particularly interested in connecting rigorous verification with machine learning to ensure correctness, safety, and interpretability of intelligent autonomous systems.

Research Vision

My research aims to establish a foundation for trustworthy autonomy by developing verification techniques that make AI-based systems reliable and provably safe. I focus on combining symbolic reasoning with data-driven learning to verify and certify cyber-physical systems such as autonomous vehicles and intelligent power grids.

I design scalable verification algorithms, bridge learning and logic through abstraction, and define formal contracts and conformance relations for neural controllers. The long-term goal is to build a verification ecosystem where engineers can design AI systems by proof, integrating safety and correctness throughout their lifecycle.
