About

Projects

Formal Verification of Camera-Based Autonomous Systems — Developed techniques to verify safety of autonomous vehicles operating in 3D environments. Introduced interval image abstraction to efficiently represent sets of images, ensuring collision-free trajectories and enabling falsification of unsafe scenarios.
Formal Reasoning of Power Grid Networks with IEDs — Created a framework for analyzing interactions between intelligent electronic devices (IEDs) and power grids. Focuses on ensuring system reliability and uncovering potential vulnerabilities in smart grid infrastructures using formal verification.
Conformance Checking for Closed-Loop Systems with DNN Controllers — Proposed approximate conformance methods to compare behaviors of different neural network controllers in closed-loop settings. Provides guarantees on performance preservation across retrained networks by reducing the problem to reachability analysis.
Formal Verification of a Separation Kernel — Designed a conditional parametric refinement framework for verifying generative separation kernels like Muen. Demonstrated correctness proofs for separation properties, contributing to secure and reliable kernel architectures.
